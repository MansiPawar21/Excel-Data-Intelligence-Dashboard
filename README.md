# 📊 Excel Data Intelligence Dashboard

### Interactive Business Analytics Dashboard Using Microsoft Excel

---

## 📖 Project Overview

The Excel Data Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive business analytics solution developed using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard transforms raw sales and business data into meaningful insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and performance analysis.

This project demonstrates advanced Excel skills, including Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Dashboard Design, and Business Intelligence reporting techniques.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze business performance through key metrics.
* Monitor sales and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Evaluate regional performance.
* Visualize payment method distribution.
* Support data-driven decision-making.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Electronics generated the highest revenue among all categories.
* Laptop emerged as the top-performing product.
* Central region contributed the highest revenue.
* Cash and Credit Card were the most preferred payment methods.
* Revenue distribution varied significantly across product categories.
